Enter the arcade

MAGWest has a giant arcade, stocked full of rhythm games, pinball, consoles, and so much more. Plus, all the machines are free-to-play. We might be a small event, but hopefully so are our lines!

Almost all of the arcade cabinets at MAGWest are brought by individual collectors. If you can get your arcade machines to the event and make it available for attendees to play, we'll give you a free three-day badge! Apply here or e-mail arcade@magwest.org with questions.

Console game til you drop

We got CONSOLES GALORE! Get yourself a genuine case of Nintendo thumb, right here at MAGWest.

Rock out!

Join us for TWO jam-packed nights of VIDEO GAME MUSIC!!!!!! Last year's lineup featured performers such as the Super Soul Bros., Kirby's Dream Band, Darren Korb, and more. Stay tuned for information on this year's performers!

Would you rather be the one making the music? Give an impromptu performance in one of our public Jam Pods or head to the Jam Space, a place to learn and make music with your new friends.

See a panel

The inaugural MAGWest had round-the-clock content run by fans, creators, and local hobos. Stay tuned for the 2018 schedule soon!

Okay, but what the heck is a MAGWest?

MAGWest is a 3-day all-hours festival celebrating the communities of people that video games can bring together, and exploring the creativity that comes with the people who are passionate about video games. Want to find out more? Contact us. Or check out last year's site.
